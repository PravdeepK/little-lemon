Shakshuka

Shakshouka is a traditional dish from the Maghrebi region made by poaching eggs in a flavorful tomato-based sauce with peppers, onions, garlic, and spices such as cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper. The sauce is often made with olive oil.

Greek Salad

Greek salad is a traditional dish in Greek cuisine that typically consists of diced tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, feta cheese, and olives, all tossed together and dressed with salt, Greek oregano, and olive oil. It is a popular choice for a light and refreshing meal.

Pita Wrap

This low carb Greek wrap is a quick and delicious meal option. It is filled with marinated chicken and authentic tzatziki sauce, making it a satisfying choice for lunch or any time you want a quick meal.

Hummus

This savory dish consists of cooked and mashed chickpeas mixed with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. It is commonly garnished with olive oil, whole chickpeas, parsley, and paprika.
